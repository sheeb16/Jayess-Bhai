Jai ho Jayess Bhai...

In the land of hush, where Jayesh roams,

A friend so quiet, in wisdom he combs.

Not many words, but a bright, smart mind,

In simplicity, his brilliance we find.

In the quiet corner of the class,

Jayesh ponders, a thoughtful mass.

His mind, a treasure trove untold,

In simple words, his wisdom unfolds.

Not loud or bold, but a gentle stream,

Jayesh's intellect, a radiant beam.

In the calm of his reflective gaze,

A silent symphony, life conveys.

With every moment, his brilliance grows,

Like a garden where knowledge flows.

A friendship formed in quiet grace,

Jayesh, a star in life's embrace.
